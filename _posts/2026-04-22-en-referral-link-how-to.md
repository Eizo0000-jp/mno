Thinking about switching your phone plan in Japan? If you've been researching Rakuten Mobile, you may have come across the term "employee referral." This guide walks you through exactly how to apply for Rakuten Mobile using an employee referral link — step by step — and explains why it's one of the smartest ways to sign up, especially if you're switching from another carrier.

## What Is the Rakuten Mobile Employee Referral Campaign?

Rakuten Mobile runs an **employee referral program** where current Rakuten employees share a special referral link with friends, family, and acquaintances. When you sign up through that link, both you and the employee receive bonus Rakuten Points.

Here's what makes this particularly attractive:

- **MNP (carrier switch):** You receive **14,000 Rakuten Points**
- **New number signup:** You receive **11,000 Rakuten Points**

Compare that to the standard campaign available to the general public, which offers **13,000 points for MNP** — the employee referral gives you **1,000 extra points** on top of that. It's a small but meaningful bonus just for using the right link.

### Step 2: Choose Your Application Type

Once you land on the Rakuten Mobile page via the referral link, you'll be prompted to choose how you want to sign up:

- **MNP (番号移行 / Number Portability):** You're keeping your current phone number and switching from another carrier such as SoftBank, docomo, or au. This earns you **14,000 points**.
- **New Number (新規申し込み):** You're getting a brand new phone number. This earns you **11,000 points**.

For most people switching from an existing plan, MNP is the right choice. ### Step 3: Log In or Create a Rakuten Account

You'll need a **Rakuten account** (楽天ID) to proceed. If you already shop on Rakuten Ichiba or use Rakuten Pay, you likely have one. Simply log in.

If you don't have an account yet, you can create one during the application process. It only takes a few minutes and requires a valid email address.

---

### Step 4: Prepare Your Documents

Before completing your application, gather the following:

- **Residence card (在留カード / Zairyu Card):** Required for identity verification as a foreign resident
- **Current carrier's MNP reservation number (MNP予約番号):** Required only if you're doing MNP. You can request this from your current carrier via their app, website, or customer service line. It typically expires in 15 days, so request it just before you apply.
- **Payment method:** Credit or debit card, or you can set up bank transfer (口座振替)
- **Compatible SIM-unlocked smartphone:** Make sure your device is unlocked and compatible with Rakuten Mobile's network (Band 3 / Band 18 support recommended)

---

### Step 5: Complete the Online Application Form

Fill in your personal details as prompted:

- Name (as listed on your residence card)
- Address
- Date of birth
- Contact phone number

The form is available in Japanese, but many of the fields are straightforward. If you need help navigating the Japanese interface, using a browser with auto-translation enabled (such as Chrome) can make this much easier.

---

### Step 6: eKYC Identity Verification

Rakuten Mobile uses **eKYC (electronic Know Your Customer)** verification, which means you verify your identity digitally using your smartphone camera. You'll be asked to:

1. Take a photo of your **residence card** (front and back)
2. Take a **selfie** or short video to confirm it matches

This step is usually completed within a few minutes and is entirely done through the Rakuten Mobile app or browser interface.

---

### Step 7: SIM or eSIM Delivery

After your application is approved, you choose between:

- **Physical SIM card:** Shipped to your registered address, typically arrives within 2–3 business days
- **eSIM:** Available instantly after approval if your device supports it — great if you want to start using Rakuten Mobile right away

If you're switching via MNP, your number will transfer over once you activate the new SIM and complete the switch in the app.

---

### Step 8: Activate Your SIM and Log Into the Rakuten Link App

Once your SIM arrives or your eSIM is set up, activate it through the **My Rakuten Mobile app**. After activation, download and log into the **Rakuten Link app** — this is important because Rakuten Link is used for free domestic calls and is tied to your point eligibility.

## When Will You Receive Your Points?

This is a common question, so it's worth being clear:

Points are **not paid immediately**. They are distributed in **3 installments**, beginning **4 months after the month in which the referral link was accessed**.

For example, if you clicked the referral link in May, your first installment would arrive in September. The remaining two installments follow in subsequent months. Make sure to keep your line active during this period, as canceling early may disqualify you from receiving remaining points.
