Simply holding a Rakuten Mobile line gives you a **+4 points multiplier** on Rakuten Ichiba purchases (conditions apply). This means every time you shop online — for groceries, electronics, daily goods — you're earning points at a much higher rate than a non-mobile user.

...your SPU multiplier can climb significantly, making every purchase on Rakuten Ichiba work much harder for you.

For foreign residents who shop online frequently — especially those who use Rakuten for international shipping services or Japan-specific goods — this multiplier effect is a real, recurring benefit.

## Practical Tips to Maximize Your Rakuten Ecosystem Savings

Here are some actionable steps to get the most out of your Rakuten Mobile subscription as a foreign resident:

### 1. Link Rakuten Mobile to Your Rakuten Card
Pay your monthly plan fee using your Rakuten Card to earn points on the payment itself. This creates a small but consistent loop of point accumulation every month.

### 2. Activate Rakuten Link for Free International Calls
The Rakuten Link app allows you to make free calls within Japan and to certain international destinations. For foreign residents who regularly call family overseas, this alone can save thousands of yen per month.

### 3. Set Rakuten Ichiba as Your Default Online Shopping Stop
Once your SPU is elevated by your mobile plan, shopping at Rakuten Ichiba becomes noticeably more rewarding. Even occasional purchases add up meaningfully over a year.

### 4. Use Rakuten Pay at Physical Stores
Rakuten Pay is widely accepted at convenience stores, pharmacies, and supermarkets across Japan. Using it in combination with your Rakuten Card and Rakuten Mobile status maximizes points earned on everyday spending.

### 5. Time Your MNP During Bonus Campaigns
Rakuten Mobile periodically runs campaigns that stack on top of base referral bonuses. Keep an eye on the official site for seasonal boosts, especially around New Year, Golden Week, and summer.
