## What Is the Rakuten Mobile Employee Referral Campaign?

If you're a foreign resident in Japan looking for a reliable, affordable mobile plan, you've probably come across Rakuten Mobile. But did you know that signing up through an **employee referral link** can get you significantly more Rakuten Points than the standard campaign?

The **Rakuten Saikyou Plan employee referral points campaign** is one of the best-kept secrets for saving money when switching to Rakuten Mobile. Instead of just going directly to the website, using a referral link from a Rakuten employee gives you an instant bonus — and the numbers are quite compelling.

---

## How Many Points Can You Actually Get?

Here's the breakdown of what you can earn:

### Via Employee Referral Link
- **MNP (switching from another carrier):** 14,000 Rakuten Points
- **New number signup:** 11,000 Rakuten Points

### Standard Campaign (No Referral)
- **MNP:** 13,000 Rakuten Points

That means using a referral link gives you **1,000 extra points on top of the regular MNP campaign bonus** — essentially free money, just for clicking the right link.

For foreign residents who are already using Rakuten Ichiba, Rakuten Pay, or other services in the Rakuten ecosystem, these points are genuinely useful. You can spend them on groceries, electronics, travel bookings, and much more.

## When Will You Receive the Points?

This is an important detail that many people overlook. The Rakuten Points from the employee referral campaign are **not paid all at once**. Here's how the schedule works:

- Points are distributed in **3 separate installments**
- Payments begin **4 months after the month in which you used the referral link to log in**
- Each installment arrives roughly one month apart

So if you sign up in May, your first installment of points would arrive around September or October. It's a staggered system, but the total amount is still the same — and it's fully worth the wait.

Make sure you log into your Rakuten account using the referral link **before** completing the application. The timing of that login is what determines when the point distribution schedule starts.

## How to Apply – Step by Step

Here's a simple walkthrough of the application process when using the employee referral link.

### Step 1: Access the Referral Link

Click on the employee referral URL:
👉 [https://r10.to/henTIE](https://r10.to/henTIE)

This will take you to the Rakuten Mobile campaign page. Make sure you're logged into your Rakuten account **on this page** before proceeding.

### Step 2: Choose Your Plan

Select the **Rakuten Saikyou Plan**. There is only one main plan, so this step is straightforward.

### Step 3: Select MNP or New Number

- If you're switching from another carrier (SoftBank, au, docomo, etc.), choose **MNP** to qualify for the 14,000-point bonus.
- If you want a brand-new number, choose **new application** for the 11,000-point bonus.

### Step 4: Prepare Your Documents

For foreign residents, you will typically need:
- Residence card (在留カード)
- Passport (in some cases)
- Credit card or bank account for billing
- MNP reservation number (if transferring your number — request this from your current carrier before applying)

### Step 5: Complete the Application

Follow the on-screen instructions to submit your application. The SIM or eSIM will typically arrive within a few business days for physical SIM orders, or can be activated almost immediately if you choose eSIM.
